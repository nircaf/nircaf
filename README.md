## 🧠 About Me

I'm a **Neuroscientist** and **Data Scientist** working at the intersection of neuroscience, biofeedback, and applied AI — translating brain research into real-world systems.

<details>
<summary><b>⚡ What I focus on (click to expand)</b></summary>
<br>

| Domain | Focus |
|:---|:---|
| 🔬 **Epilepsy & Blood-Brain Barrier** | Imaging BBB dysfunction in drug-resistant epilepsy across multiple clinical centers |
| 🧬 **Dementia & Neuroinflammation** | Biomarker development and early-stage diagnostic tooling |
| 🎮 **Brain-Computer Interaction** | Adaptive systems driven by real-time EEG signals |
| 📡 **EEG & Biofeedback** | Open-source dashboards and analysis pipelines for neural data |
| 🤖 **Applied AI** | Machine learning pipelines that turn neuroscience data into clinical insights |

</details>
